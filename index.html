About Us
SweetCrumbs Bakery began in 2010 as a small family kitchen in Nairobi, driven by
a simple mission: to bring warm, handmade baked goods to every neighborhood table.
Today, we still mix every batch of dough by hand and bake fresh daily, staying true
to the recipes our founder started with over a decade ago. Our mission is to serve
quality, affordability, and a little bit of joy in every bite.
